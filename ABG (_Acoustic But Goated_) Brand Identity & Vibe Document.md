# **ABG ("Acoustic But Goated") Brand Identity & Vibe Document**

## **1\. The Core Identity**

*This section defines who you are, what you stand for, and the cultural flip you are executing.*

* **Brand Name:** ABG  
* **The Meaning:** "Acoustic But Goated" (A deliberate, ironic flip on the traditional "Asian Baby Girl" trope).  
* **The Elevator Pitch:** ABG means Asian Baby Girl? Nah. For us, it’s Acoustic But Goated. Right now, students walking around Samyan are basically forced into an NPC uniform—standard faculty jerseys or the exact same Uniqlo tees as everyone else. So we built ABG. We are creating the unofficial 'Samyan Baddie' uniform. It’s effortless everyday wear, but injected with bold, limited-run aesthetics. Our obsession is premium construction—utilizing the same heavyweight fabrics as luxury streetwear labels, but without the gatekept price tag. Plus, we custom-built the backend tech ourselves, so when our drops go live, the checkout is flawless and you actually get your gear without the usual merch-pickup chaos.  
* **The Mission:** To create “baddy/ it-girl/ it-guy” aesthetic clothing for the modern university student—merch that feels deeply embedded in campus culture, inside jokes, and the student hustle.

## **2\. The Aesthetic Collision**

*This section defines the visual tension of the brand. You are combining two very different worlds.*

* **The Foundation (The "Brandy Melville" Element):** \* **Vibe:** Effortless, cult-favorite, minimalist, and highly wearable everyday basics.  
  * **Silhouettes:** Long Baby Tee, Oversized Tee, Heavy Hoodie  
* **The Edge:**  
  * **Vibe:** Funny, Brain rot, aesthetically pleasing, Pinterest girly , loud, community-gated, and aggressive.  
  * **Design Details:** heavy puff-print graphics / Clean DTG, bold hyper-specific typography, thick heavyweight fabrics.  
* **The Synthesis:** "Comfortable enough to sleep in after a late-night study session, but bold enough to be the focal point of a fit pic."

## **3\. The "Samyan Baddie" Archetype (Target Audience)**

*Who is the exact person wearing this? Give them a specific persona so every design decision filters through them.*

* **The Persona:** The "Samyan Baddie" / "SAMYAN BAD DIH".  
* **Their Lifestyle:** They are constantly balancing academic pressure with a highly active social life around the campus. They might be managing an organization's PR, running a campus club, or just surviving midterms.  
* **Their Media Diet:** High screen time on TikTok, deeply fluent in meme culture and Gen-Z slang, immune to traditional polished advertising.  
* **What They Value:** Authenticity, localized exclusivity (wearing something nobody outside their specific scene has), and high-quality garments that don't feel like generic campus bookstore merch.

## **4\. Brand Voice & Tone**

*How the brand speaks in captions, on the website, and in emails.*

* **Tone Words:** Ironic, raw, hyper-aware, chaotic but calculated.  
* **The Vibe:** Speaks like a peer, not a corporation.  
* **Copywriting Rules:**   
  * *Rule 1:* No standard marketing speak (Never say: "Shop our new high-quality collection today\!").  
  * *Rule 2:* Lean into vulnerability and the messy reality of running a brand (Say: "We literally fought our print-on-demand supplier to get this puff-print right. Only 50 available.").  
  * *Rule 3:* Keep it localized. Reference specific campus locations, weather, or student experiences.

## **5\. Visual Language & Photography**

*The strict visual rules that will make an ABG post recognizable within 0.8 seconds of scrolling.*

* **Color Palette:** \#F5F1E8, \#3F3F44

* **Typography:** Helvetica, HK Grotesk Wide  
* **Photography Style:** Zero studio lighting. Everything should look like it was taken on a digital camera or iPhone on the streets, near the university, or in a messy dorm room. Flash photography, slightly blurred motion shots, and raw behind-the-scenes content.

## **6\. Product & Drop Philosophy**

*How the actual physical items are treated and released.*

* **Quality Standard:** Moving away from cheap promotional blanks. Focusing on specific cuts, washes, and reliable print infrastructure to ensure the pieces survive a hundred washes.  
* **The Drop Mechanics:** High-velocity, limited runs. Cultivating a feeling that if you don't secure the "SAMYAN BADDIE" drop this week, you will definitely miss out on the cultural moment.
